How to Participate

  1. Travel to a dark location on a clear night, far from city lights, to minimize light pollution.
  2. Let your eyes adjust to the darkness for 10â€“15 minutes to improve visibility of faint stars.
  3. Use a stargazing app or star chart to locate a major constellation such as Orion or Leo.
  4. Count the number of visible stars within the chosen constellation.
  5. Record the date, time, weather conditions, and your precise location (GPS coordinates, if possible).
  6. Submit your observation using the form below to contribute to the community database.
